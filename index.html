My Story

I’m Divyanshu Negi, a passionate data analyst eager to turn raw data into meaningful insights.

My journey began with curiosity — I’ve always been drawn to finding patterns, solving problems, and understanding the “why” behind numbers. This curiosity led me to explore tools like Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI, where I learned to clean, analyze, and visualize data effectively.

Through personal and academic projects, I’ve developed a strong foundation in data storytelling, dashboard creation, and data-driven decision-making. I enjoy transforming messy datasets into clear visual insights that make sense to everyone.

I may be at the start of my professional journey, but my passion for data and continuous learning keeps me moving forward — turning information into impact, one dataset at a time.
